Festival Info

GTM will land in Canberra at the University of Canberra, Kirinari St Bruce ACT 2617, on Sun 24 April 2016 Gates open at 10:30am with the first bands taking to the stage around 11:00am and the last band finished around 10:30pm. Please find the Canberra Playing Times here. Download our app here.

Parking

There is limited parking available at The University of Canberra from 9:30am until late. Please follow the signs and enter via College Street or Allawoona Street. Parking at UC is free.

Drop Off Areas

Being dropped off at the Canberra Moo by a friend or family member? There will be a designated public drop off and pick up zone on Gininderra Drive. For your own safety please follow the directional signage and instructions from traffic controllers and police officers.

Taxi info

The Taxi rank at GTM Canberra is located at the car park opposite the main entry on Allawoona Street. Contact Canberra Cabs on: 132 227

Food

GTM will have a diverse selection of mouth watering catering options to give you the sustenance you need to make it through till the end of the night! With over 20 food stalls there is will be something to cater to all tastes. From sweet to savoury, from local providers to festival favourites (yes, that includes gozleme!) peppered with a selection of International tastes.

Water

Its very important to keep hydrated throughout the day. There will be free water stations at GTM so you can either fill up the empty water bottle you brought along with you OR refill the water bottle you purchased from the bar or food outlet. For a full list of items you can bring into the festival, check out the Conditions of Entry tab on our Festival Info page.

Beverages

A range of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages including beer, cider, spirits, soft drinks, juice and flavoured milk will be available from outlets around the event. GTM will again be running a recycling program for the Bars.

Merchandise

Pay a visit to GTMs Merch tent to grab a memento from this years festival. There will be a range of goodies from a stubby holder to keep your drink cool to your favourite bands t-shirt. Take your T-shirt along to GTMs Signing Tent where a selection of Artists from the tour will be signing for GTM fans.

Eftpos / ATMs

Remember to bring cash. ATMs will be onsite at GTM, however these are always in high demand and can be slow. Best bet is to get organised BEFORE you get to GTM and bring enough cash to get you through the day. Nobody wants to stand in a line when you could be watching your favourite band.

Information headspace helpdesk

The headspace helpdesk is back again this year to help answer any questions you have about GTM or getting home. Look for the bright green and head over and talk to the friendly team.

Lost & Found

The headspace helpdesk is the place to go to hand in a lost item youve found at the event or to see if someone has handed in an item that you or a friend has lost at the event. Items not collected at the end of the night: Will be retained at the event site by GTM for two (2) days post event. Stay tuned for collection info.

First Aid

A First Aid tent will be operation from GTMs start to finish and is the place to go if you or one of your friends isnt feeling well. Not sure whats wrong? Or think maybe you just need a rest? Better to be on the safe side and talk to our First Aid operators  theyll be able to help or send you along to someone else who can.

Cloakroom

GTM will be running a Cloakroom for your convenience, check out the details below;

Cost: One (1) standard cloak is $5.00

What is one (1) standard cloak?

  • 1 article of clothing per hanger
  • Loose items must be able to fit into the equivalent of 1 x A4 Bag
  • 1 x personal bag no larger than a standard day pack

Anyone wishing to cloak items larger than one (1) standard cloak must purchase a second cloak.

Valuables (including mobile phones, wallets, cameras and electrical equipment) will be cloaked at the risk of the customer and must be listed on the cloak tag at the time of cloaking the item. GTM will not take responsibility for items not declared at the time of cloaking.

Customers are responsible for ensuring they retain their wristband / cloak tag throughout the day. Lost wristbands / cloak tags will be asked to wait until the end of the evening to collect items

Accessing your stuff throughout the day: You can access your stuff throughout the day for the cost of $2.00 by way of presenting your wristband / cloak tag.

Cloaked items not collected at the end of the night: Will be retained at the event site by GTM for two (2) days post event. Stay tuned for collection info.
